// 01 — ABOUT

Our Mission

Matfyz Quant Club was born from a shared curiosity: what happens when mathematics, computer science, and financial theory collide? What began as informal discussions among students has grown into a structured community — one where members teach each other, challenge each other, and build real skills together.

Our approach is inspired by Feynman's method: if you can explain a concept simply, you truly understand it. Through peer-teaching and collaborative exploration, we believe every member can reach a deeper level of mastery.

Open to all years and backgrounds — curiosity is the only prerequisite.

"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."

— Albert Einstein

// 02 — PRINCIPLES

Core Principles

Learn Together, Grow Together

We learn by teaching. Every session is an opportunity to share knowledge, ask questions, and grow collectively as a community.

Open to All

No gatekeeping — whether you're a first-year student or a PhD candidate, there's a place for you here. All fields and levels of experience welcome.

The Feynman Method

True understanding means being able to explain ideas from first principles. We use this as our standard for mastery across every topic we tackle.

// 03 — FOCUS AREAS

What We Study

Quantitative Finance

We explore the mathematical foundations that power modern financial markets.

Machine Learning

Statistical learning, neural networks, and algorithmic models applied to real-world financial and scientific data problems.

Data Science

Extracting insights from complex datasets using rigorous statistical methods, visualization, and computational tools.

Mathematics

Probability theory, linear algebra, optimization — the bedrock of everything we do. We go back to fundamentals and build from there.

// 04 — HOW WE WORK

Two Pillars

The Problem-Solving Gym

Weekly sessions focused on sharpening analytical skills. We work through puzzles, algorithm-style questions, and brainteasers drawn from real quant interviews.

  • Weekly problem-solving sessions
  • Puzzles & quant brainteasers
  • Algorithm-style questions
  • Periodic assessment tests

The Applied Project Lab

Where theory meets practice. We participate in hackathons, run internal research projects, and collaborate with industry partners on real quantitative challenges.

  • Hackathons
  • Internal group research projects
  • Junior & senior tracks
  • Industry collaboration
